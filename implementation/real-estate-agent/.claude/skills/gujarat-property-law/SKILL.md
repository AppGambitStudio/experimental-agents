## RERA Act 2016 (Gujarat)
- All projects with plot area > 500 sqm OR > 8 units MUST be registered with GujRERA
- Unregistered projects are ILLEGAL to sell — this is a deal-breaker
- Registration must be active (not expired) at the time of purchase
- Builder must deposit 70% of buyer payments in a separate RERA escrow account
- Complaints can be filed on the GujRERA portal — check complaint count as a builder signal

## NA (Non-Agricultural) Conversion
- Agricultural land in Gujarat CANNOT be used for residential/commercial without NA permission
- NA order must come from the Collector's office
- Without NA, any construction is ILLEGAL and cannot be registered
- Check 7/12 extract on AnyRoR for land use classification

## Title Transfer Rules
- Complete chain of title from original owner to current seller is essential
- Gaps in title chain = potential disputes
- Verify via: registered sale deeds (GARVI), mutation entries (AnyRoR), 7/12 extract
- Minimum 30-year title search recommended

## Stamp Duty (Gujarat)
- Residential: 3.5% of property value (or jantri value, whichever is higher)
- Commercial: 4.9% of property value
- Registration fee: 1% (capped at Rs 30,000)
- Female first-time buyer discount: Rs 10,000 off registration fee
- E-stamping available via SHCIL/Stock Holding Corporation

## Cash Component Risks
- Cash payments are illegal under Income Tax Act and Benami Transaction Act
- Buyer loses legal recourse for the cash portion
- Future capital gains tax calculated on declared (lower) value = higher tax later
- Stamp duty calculated on jantri rate regardless — no savings on stamp duty from cash
